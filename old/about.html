About

Hello there!

My name is Victoria, I use they/them and she/her pronouns,
and I'm an educator, writer, and engineer.

I'm a proud Chapina (Guatemalan) born and raised in Chicago,
now calling Providence (RI) home.

I attended Brown University, where I earned a Master's in
Urban Education Policy, with a focus on Computer Science Education,
as well as a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Hispanic Studies.

I'm passionate about accessibility,
STEM education (particularly CS education), and civic tech.

Outside of computer science and teaching, I enjoy reading,
writing (poetry and short stories), and watching
anything that's suspense, thriller, or comedy.

I am a proud QuestBridge Scholar, Technolochica,
and a member of NCWIT Aspirations, among others.
